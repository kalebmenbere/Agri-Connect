Privacy Policy & Terms

At Agri Connect, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. We collect only the necessary data to provide better services and do not share your information with third parties without consent.

  • Prohibited Activities: Misuse, fraud, or any illegal activities are strictly prohibited.
  • Service Availability: We strive for 24/7 service availability and this website is only applicable within the Ethiopian region..
  • Liability: Agri Connect is not responsible for third-party transactions or external vendor interactions.

We strive to provide the best service, but in case of issues, our sales and refund policies are as follows:

  • Payment: All transactions are securely processed.
  • Order Cancellation: Cancellations are allowed within 24 hours of order placement.
  • Refunds: Refunds are issued for valid cases, such as undelivered or defective products.
  • Disputes: Any disputes should be reported within 7 days of the transaction.

  • Farmers can only register and cannot edit their products; only the admin has editing privileges.
  • Farmers will be notified when there is an order from a buyer. However, the buyer has a 1-hour interval to complete the online payment. If the buyer does not complete the payment, the product will be returned to the product list.
  • If the buyer completes the purchase, the payment will be reflected on the "Paid" page, confirming the farmer has received the money in their bank account.

  • Buyers can only view products if they are signed in.
  • Orders are processed on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • After successful payment, buyers will receive their products within 5-7 days, depending on their location.
